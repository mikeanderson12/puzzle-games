üìñ How to Play

Objective: Watch the sequence of colors and repeat it correctly to advance to the next level!

How to Play:

  1. Click the START button to begin
  2. Watch carefully as Simon lights up a sequence of colors
  3. After the sequence finishes, repeat it by clicking the colored buttons in the same order
  4. Each level adds one more color to the sequence
  5. The game ends if you make a mistake

Tips:

  • Pay close attention to both the visual and audio cues
  • Try to remember the pattern as a rhythm or melody
  • Stay focused - the sequences get longer with each level!
  • Take your time - there's no rush when repeating the sequence

Scoring:

  • Each correct button press earns you 1 point
  • Complete a level to advance and face a longer sequence
  • Try to beat your high score!
